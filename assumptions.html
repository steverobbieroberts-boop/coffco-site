Assumptions and Dependencies

9 assumptions · 5 dependencies · conditions required for the system to operate as intended

Source: SRS V3.0 Assumptions & Dependencies sheet Assumptions: 9 Dependencies: 5
Assumptions are conditions taken to be true for the purposes of this programme, which have not been formally verified. Dependencies are external items or decisions that must be in place for the system to achieve its requirements. Both should be reviewed at each design gate.
IDCategoryTypeDescription
A-01Power supplyAssumptionVacation Stables hotel rooms have standard UK/EU 230V/50Hz or US 110V/60Hz mains power sockets available at or near the room desk.
A-02NetworkAssumptionHotel Wi-Fi is available in rooms, required if IoT features are included in MVP scope.
A-03Water supplyAssumptionMains water supply is available in each hotel room for guest use; connection to machine water supply is via a removable user-filled reservoir — not a direct plumbing connection.
A-04HousekeepingAssumptionVacation Stables housekeeping staff perform daily room servicing and are available to restock consumables and perform basic cleaning of the machine as part of their normal duties.
A-05Mug provisionAssumptionVacation Stables provides guests with their standard mug (100mm H × 100mm D) in the room.
A-06Consumable supplyAssumptionCoffCo or its designated supply partner provides an uninterrupted supply of compatible coffee consumables to all Vacation Stables properties.
A-07Room footprintAssumptionSufficient desk or surface space of at least 300mm × 300mm is available in each room for machine placement.
A-08Regulatory complianceAssumptionCoffCo's engineering team will obtain all necessary product certifications (CE marking EU, UKCA UK, UL/ETL US) before commercial deployment.
A-09Milk refrigerationAssumptionThe machine incorporates an integrated milk refrigeration store maintaining milk at ≤4°C — required for frothed milk production without guest-supplied fresh milk.
D-01User requirementsDependencyThe Vacation Stables User Requirements Document (CONOPS & URD v1.0) remains the authoritative statement of customer need throughout the programme.
D-02Vacation Stables PMSDependencyIf guest loyalty profile features are included in a future increment, this system depends on API access to the Vacation Stables Property Management System.
D-03Consumable compatibilityDependencySystem performance requirements assume use of CoffCo-specified coffee consumables. Third-party consumables may not meet extraction pressure or volume requirements.
D-04InfrastructureDependencyThe 365-day maintenance-free target is dependent on Vacation Stables providing water hardness data per property. Where water hardness exceeds threshold, descale interval will be shorter.
D-05Sustainability targetsDependencyThe recycled material and recyclable waste requirements depend on CoffCo's supply chain partners confirming material composition and end-of-life routes before deployment.
