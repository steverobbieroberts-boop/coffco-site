TC-001 Brew temperature — americano
SRS 4.1		 T 2–4 Brew americano into insulated vessel, measure temperature with calibrated probe immediately at dispensation. ≥60°C
TC-002 Milk temperature range
SRS 4.2/4.3		 T 2–4 Steam milk to hot frothed state, measure immediately at dispensation. 60–90°C
TC-003 Cycle time
SRS 3.1/4.11		 T 2–4 Time from first user input (button press) to beverage ready at 400 ml worst case. ≤180 s
TC-004 Unaided first-time operation
SRS 2.1		 D 4–6 5 naive test subjects with no briefing attempt americano production. No documentation permitted. ≥4/5 succeed unaided
TC-008 Dry-fire prevention
SRS 1.10		 T 2 Empty reservoir. Initiate brew cycle. Measure heater element current. Confirm FLUID-EMPTY state and auto-recovery. 0.0 A heater; FLUID-EMPTY within 5 s; auto-recovery on refill
TC-HE-01 Hardware thermal cutoff
SRS 3.10		 T 2 Apply controlled overtemperature. Confirm TCO opens at trip point. TCO opens at ≥100°C; heater de-energised
TC-FW-01 Firmware watchdog reset
SRS 3.12		 T 2 Halt firmware heartbeat deliberately. Confirm WDT IC asserts RESET and de-energises actuators. RESET within 500 ms; all actuators de-energised
TC-PRV-01 Pressure relief valve
SRS 3.13		 I+T 2 Pressurise fluid path incrementally. Confirm PRV opens at limit and vents to drip tray only. PRV opens ≤2.5 bar; vent to drip tray only; non-venting ≤1.3 bar
TC-IPX2-01 Enclosure IP rating
SRS 3.11		 T 2+4 Apply IPX2 drip test per IEC 60529. No water ingress to live parts; all functions nominal post-test
TC-ST-01 Surface temperature and stability
SRS 3.10/4.10		 T+I 4 Run full brew cycle. Measure all accessible external surfaces. Apply 50 N lateral force. No surface >48°C; stable under 50 N lateral
TC-OTA-01 Scheduled OTA firmware update
SRS 1.11		 T 3–4 Test all 5 OTA conditions: night-window only, STANDBY state-gate, 30 min activity-free, rollback on failed reboot, UPDATE-COMPLETE telemetry. All 5 conditions pass
