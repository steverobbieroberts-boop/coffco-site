Operating Environment

Physical · Usage · Regulatory · Commercial constraints on the CoffCo system

Source: SRS V3.0 Operating Environment sheet Categories: Physical, Usage, Regulatory, Commercial Factors: 22
The operating environment is not a requirement. It is the context that requirements must respond to. Every SRS requirement can be traced back to one or more of the environmental factors listed here — this is what makes the requirements necessary rather than arbitrary.
Physical
Deployment location
In-room hotel guest room — desk or bedside surface. Standard hotel furniture with no modifications permitted.
Footprint constraint
Maximum 300mm (W) × 300mm (D) × 300mm (H). Must fit existing desk space without displacing other room items.
Power supply (EU)
230V / 50Hz mains supply. CEE 7/4 or equivalent socket standard. System must operate natively without adapters.
Power supply (US)
110V / 60Hz mains supply. Must operate natively on US supply without modification or adapters.
Water hardness
Variable — soft to very hard — across the Vacation Stables UK, EU, and US estate. Machine must accommodate the full range without performance degradation.
Thermal
Ambient room temperature 18–26°C. System processes water at up to 90°C internally. All external surfaces ≤48°C under normal operation.
Usage
Primary use case
Business executive — morning coffee upon waking. User is time-pressured, may be fatigued, has zero prior training with this machine.
Secondary use case
Afternoon coffee — lower urgency, same user profile.
Operator
Housekeeping staff — consumable restocking and cleaning. No technical training. Interaction must require no more than 5 minutes and no tools.
Usage frequency
Daily use during occupied stays. Machine may be idle for extended periods between stays — design must accommodate dormancy without performance loss.
User familiarity
Zero — no instruction material, no prior training. Machine must be fully self-explanatory from first contact.
Regulatory
Electrical safety
IEC 60335 — household and similar electrical appliances. CE marking (EU); UL/ETL listing (US); UKCA (UK). Type-test certificate required before deployment.
Food safety
HACCP principles apply to all water and beverage contact surfaces. All food-contact materials must be food-grade certified.
Energy efficiency
EU energy efficiency rating A required. Ecodesign and Energy Labelling Regulations apply.
EMC
Must not cause harmful interference to in-room devices (Wi-Fi, TV, telephone, laptop). EN 55014 / CISPR 14 compliance required.
Data protection
GDPR applies if guest usage data is processed. Minimum data principle applies — collect only what is operationally necessary.
WEEE / RoHS
All materials must be WEEE-compliant and RoHS-compliant. End-of-life recycling routes confirmed before deployment.
Commercial
Interior design
Vacation Stables standard — predominantly black or transparent. Minimum 80% of visible surface area must be black or transparent (SRS 3.6).
Mug compatibility
Existing Vacation Stables mug: 100mm height × 100mm diameter. Machine dispensing outlet must accommodate this mug with ≥105mm clearance.
Sustainability — materials
Minimum 50% of machine materials to be recycled content. Vacation Stables 2030 sustainability target.
Sustainability — waste
All waste products generated during operation must have a feasible recycling or reuse route. Zero landfill target (SRS 3.7).
Maintenance-free period
365 days minimum before any scheduled maintenance is required. Hotel environment means any maintenance call has brand and cost implications.
Anti-theft
Machine is a capital asset in an unsecured room. Opportunistic theft to be deterred by mechanical means — no permanent room modification.
