CLOUD PLATFORM
Slide 8

The machine is the product. The data is the business.

Wireless maintenance alerts
Real-time fault detection transmitted to CoffCo cloud ops. Corrective maintenance dispatched before the guest notices.
Predictive maintenance
Scale accumulation modelled from brew-cycle telemetry. Service scheduled before threshold breach — not after failure.
OTA firmware updates
Firmware delivered over-the-air. No engineer visit required for software fixes or feature additions.
Fleet analytics
Usage data, brew patterns, and consumable demand rates aggregated across the Vacation Stables estate in real time.
